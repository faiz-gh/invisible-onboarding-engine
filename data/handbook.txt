DERIV GLOBAL EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK (2026 EDITION)

1. REMOTE WORK POLICY
Employees are permitted to work remotely for up to 4 weeks (20 working days) per calendar year outside their primary employment country. 
- Eligibility: All full-time employees after passing probation.
- Process: Requests must be submitted via the HR Portal at least 14 days in advance.
- Restrictions: Working from sanctioned countries is strictly prohibited. Time zone overlap of at least 4 hours with your core team is required.

2. EXPENSE POLICY
Deriv is committed to providing employees with the tools they need.
- Coworking Spaces: Reimbursable up to $300 USD/month if no Deriv office is available in your current city.
- Learning & Development: Every employee has an annual budget of $1,000 USD for courses, books, and certifications. Manager approval is required for expenses over $200.
- Wellness Allowance: $50 USD/month for gym memberships, fitness apps, or mental health support.
- Meals: Daily meals are not reimbursable unless traveling for client business (Cap: $50 USD/day).
- Home Office Setup: A one-time stipend of $500 USD is provided upon hiring for monitors, chairs, or desks.

3. LEAVE POLICY (GLOBAL BASELINE)
We believe in work-life balance.
- Annual Leave: 30 calendar days per year.
- Sick Leave: 10 days fully paid per year. A doctor's note is required after 3 consecutive days of absence.
- Compassionate Leave: 5 days paid leave for the loss of an immediate family member.
- Sabbatical: Eligible after 5 years of continuous service. You are entitled to 4 weeks of fully paid sabbatical leave.

4. RELOCATION & VISA SUPPORT
For international hires moving more than 500km to a Deriv office location:
- Relocation Allowance: A one-time payment of $5,000 USD (net) is provided with your first month's salary to cover moving costs, flights, and temporary accommodation.
- Visa Sponsorship: Deriv covers 100% of visa application fees for the employee and their immediate dependents (spouse and children).
- Temporary Housing: Up to 2 weeks of hotel accommodation is provided upon arrival.

5. PROBATION & NOTICE PERIODS
- Probation Period: Standard probation is 6 months.
- Notice Period (During Probation): 1 week.
- Notice Period (After Probation): 1 month for individual contributors, 2 months for management roles.

6. CORE VALUES
- Integrity: We do the right thing, always.
- Competence: We are good at what we do and never stop learning.
- Customer Focus: We treat our clients the way we want to be treated.
